Geospatial Software Engineering

Our Geospatial Software Engineering division at Matogen leads the way in providing innovative solutions for geospatial data analysis and visualization.

Backed by a team of highly skilled engineers and GIS experts, we specialize in delivering customized software applications that encompass mapping, spatial analysis, remote sensing, and geodatabase management.

Our unwavering commitment to harnessing advanced technologies empowers clients to extract valuable insights from geospatial data, empowering them to make well-informed decisions.

Our exceptional team comprises seasoned specialist software developers, many boasting over 15+ years of extensive commercial software development experience.

Technology stacks typically used include:

  • C#
  • Net Standard
  • Net Core
  • Net Framework
  • Asp.Net MVC
  • Asp.Net WebApi
  • Unity3D
  • MS-SQL
  • MongoDB
  • Azure
  • AWS
  • Angular
  • jQuery
